# Billing & Subscription

CuibOS uses **Stripe** for payment processing. All transactions are secure and PCI DSS compliant.

## Available plans

| Plan | Price | Properties | Vehicles | Members |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Free | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| **Pro** | 29 RON/month | 5 | 3 | 3 |
| **Pro Plus** | 59 RON/month | 25 | 10 | 10 |

## Upgrading your plan

::steps

### Access the billing page

From the sidebar → **Billing** or from the profile menu.

### Select the desired plan

Compare plans and click **Upgrade** on the desired one.

### Complete payment

You will be redirected to Stripe Checkout. Enter your card details and confirm.

### Instant activation

After confirmed payment, the plan activates instantly. New limits apply immediately.

::

## Downgrading your plan

Downgrade is also done from the billing page. New limits apply at the end of the current billing period.

::alert{type="warning"}
When downgrading, if you have more resources than the new plan allows (e.g., 5 properties on Free), the extra ones will be automatically **locked**.
::

## Stripe Portal

From the billing page, you can access the **Stripe Portal** to:
- View invoices
- Update payment method
- Cancel subscription
- Download receipts

## Payment flow during registration

If you choose a paid plan during registration:

1. Complete payment details on Stripe Checkout
2. After confirmed payment, complete the registration form (email, password)
3. Verify your email and log in
4. Your account starts on the chosen plan directly
