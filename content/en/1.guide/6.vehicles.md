# Vehicles

The vehicles module helps you track all family vehicles: cars, motorcycles, etc.

## Adding a vehicle

From the sidebar → **Vehicles** → **Add vehicle**. Fill in:

- **Make** and **model**
- **Year of manufacture**
- **Registration number**
- **VIN** (optional)
- **Fuel type** — petrol, diesel, electric, hybrid, LPG
- **Engine capacity** (optional)
- **Color** (optional)

## Vehicle expenses

Record any vehicle-related expense:

- **Fuel** — fill-ups with quantity, price/liter, km
- **Service** — repairs, inspections
- **Insurance** — mandatory insurance, comprehensive
- **Taxes** — MOT, vehicle tax, road tax
- **Other** — car wash, parking, fines

Each expense is saved with date, amount and optionally notes.

## Mileage (Odometer)

Periodically record the vehicle mileage. CuibOS automatically calculates average consumption based on fill-ups and mileage.

## Planned maintenance

Define maintenance items (e.g., oil change, tire change) with:
- **Km interval** — how often in km
- **Time interval** — how often in months
- **Last performed** — date and km of last operation

### Maintenance logs

Each maintenance item has a **log** with the history of operations performed: date, km, cost, notes.

## Vehicle statuses

- **Active** — vehicle currently in use
- **Inactive** — vehicle temporarily parked
- **Sold** — vehicle sold (kept for historical records)

## Compliance alerts

CuibOS automatically checks due dates for:
- **MOT** — periodic technical inspection
- **Insurance** — mandatory car insurance
- **Road tax** — road usage tax

::alert{type="warning"}
Compliance alerts are checked periodically by the reminder service. Make sure you've entered the correct expiration dates.
::
