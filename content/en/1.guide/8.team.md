# Team & Roles

CuibOS supports teams with multiple members, each with different roles and permissions.

## Available roles

| Role | Description | Permissions |
|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | Account owner | Full access, can lock/unlock properties, manage subscription |
| **Admin** | Administrator | Can create/edit/delete properties and vehicles, invite members |
| **Viewer** | Viewer | Read-only access |

## Inviting a member

::steps

### Open team settings

From the profile menu → **Settings** → **Team**.

### Send the invitation

Enter the person's email and select the desired role. An invitation email is sent automatically.

### Accepting the invitation

The invited person receives an email with an acceptance link. If they don't have an account, they will be invited to register first.

::

## Managing invitations

As owner or admin, you can:

- **Resend invitation** — if the person didn't receive the email
- **Revoke invitation** — cancel a sent invitation
- **View status** — invited, active, revoked

## Plan limits

| Plan | Max members |
|---|---|
| Free | 1 (owner only) |
| Pro | 3 |
| Pro Plus | 10 |

::alert{type="warning"}
If you exceed the plan limit, you must upgrade before inviting new members.
::
