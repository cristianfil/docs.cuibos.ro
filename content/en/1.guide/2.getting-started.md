# Getting Started

## Creating your account

::steps

### Choose a plan

Go to [app.cuibos.ro](https://app.cuibos.ro) and select your desired plan: **Free**, **Pro** or **Pro Plus**.

### Sign up

Fill in the registration form with your email and password. For paid plans, you will be redirected to the Stripe payment page before completing registration.

### Verify your email

Check your inbox for the confirmation email and click the activation link.

### Log in

After verification, log in with your email and password.

::

## Dashboard navigation

After logging in, you will land on the main **Dashboard** which displays:

- Your active **properties** with meter status
- Registered **vehicles** with compliance alerts
- Current **budget** and recent expenses

## Account settings

From the profile menu (top right) you can:

- **Change password** — update your account password
- **Update profile** — change your display name
- **Manage subscription** — upgrade/downgrade from the billing page
- **Invite members** — add team members (available on Pro and Pro Plus plans)

::alert{type="warning"}
Your account email cannot be changed after registration. Contact support if you need a change.
::
