# Introduction

**CuibOS** is a web platform designed for complete household management: properties, vehicles and finances — all in one place.

## What you can do with CuibOS

::card-group
  ::card
  ---
  title: Properties
  icon: lucide:home
  ---
  Add properties (apartments, houses, garages), configure utilities (electricity, gas, water), record meter readings and monitor consumption.
  ::

  ::card
  ---
  title: Vehicles
  icon: lucide:car
  ---
  Manage the family fleet — registrations, expenses, mileage, planned maintenance and compliance alerts (MOT, insurance, road tax).
  ::

  ::card
  ---
  title: Finances
  icon: lucide:wallet
  ---
  Create family budgets, track bills and installments, check exchange rates and keep control of spending.
  ::

  ::card
  ---
  title: Notifications
  icon: lucide:bell
  ---
  Receive automatic alerts for due dates (bills, installments, MOT, insurance), meter readings and vehicle maintenance.
  ::
::
