# Vehicles Service

Prefix: `/vehicles/v1` (gateway) → `/vehicles` (internal)

All endpoints require JWT (except health and metadata).

## Vehicles

### GET /vehicles/v1/metadata

Returns available enums (fuel types, statuses, etc.).

---

### POST /vehicles/v1/

🔒 Creates a new vehicle.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/

🔒 Returns all vehicles for the tenant.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId

🔒 Returns vehicle details.

---

### PUT /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId

🔒 Updates vehicle details.

---

### PATCH /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/status

🔒 Changes vehicle status (`active`, `inactive`, `sold`).

---

### DELETE /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId

🔒 Deletes a vehicle.

---

## Expenses

### POST /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/expenses

🔒 Adds a vehicle expense.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/expenses

🔒 Returns vehicle expenses (paginated).

---

## Mileage (Odometer)

### POST /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/odometer-readings

🔒 Adds an odometer reading.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/odometer-readings

🔒 Returns odometer readings.

---

## Maintenance

### POST /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items

🔒 Creates a planned maintenance item.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items

🔒 Returns maintenance items.

---

### PATCH /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId

🔒 Updates a maintenance item.

---

### DELETE /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId

🔒 Deletes a maintenance item.

---

## Maintenance Logs

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance/logs

🔒 Returns all maintenance logs for the vehicle.

---

### POST /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId/logs

🔒 Adds a maintenance log entry.

---

### GET /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId/logs

🔒 Returns logs for a maintenance item.

---

### PATCH /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId/logs/:maintenanceLogId

🔒 Updates a log entry.

---

### DELETE /vehicles/v1/:vehicleId/maintenance-items/:maintenanceItemId/logs/:maintenanceLogId

🔒 Deletes a log entry.

---

## Internal Endpoints

These endpoints are called by the reminder service (not directly by users):

### POST /vehicles/v1/internal/reminders/compliance/verify

Checks vehicle compliance (MOT, insurance, road tax) and returns those needing attention.

---

### POST /vehicles/v1/internal/reminders/maintenance/verify

Checks planned maintenance and returns due items.
